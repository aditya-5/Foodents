Foodent

What is the best and safest way to enjoy food under the Pandemic? The best way is cooking by yourself!Don't worry if you know nothing about cooking! Foodent is here to help you!

Foodent is a very powerful website that can guide you to cook. We offer kinds of detailed recipes in all over the world! Moreover, Foodent can help you to find the most suitable food in numerous circumstances.

  • Do not have enough ingredients? Use our fridge feature to search which food be made from the only ingredients you have!
  • Do not have enough time to cook? Search the foods that can be done in minutes.
  • Do not know what to cook? Just browsing Foodent to find the one you like!

Additionally, if you want to share your recipes, you just need to upload pictures and instructions to Foodent!
